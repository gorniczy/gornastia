Constructing Ukraine in the American and Russian press discourses

The dissertation is dedicated to the analysis of the ways of constructing Ukraine in the American and Russian press discourses during the revolution named Euromaidan and in the months that followed. The aim of the analysis is to question the meaning-making practices related to Ukraine, liberate its image and enable its reconstruction through emancipation of Ukrainians themselves. The source database consists of the articles published in the two major newspapers — Moskovskij Komsomolets (Russia) and The New York Times (USA). The scope of the analysis includes cultural contexts, Ukraine as a whole as well as its regions and residents.

A smaller-scale study of Ukrainian crisis is presented in my article published by the academic journal Kultura i historia (PL).
